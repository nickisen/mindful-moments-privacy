Privacy Policy – Mindful Moments

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Mindful Moments is a Chrome extension that delivers hourly breathing reminders and guided meditation sessions. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

Mindful Moments collects no personal data. The only information stored is a single timestamp (lastShown) saved locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This value is used exclusively to prevent duplicate reminder popups. It is never transmitted, shared, or accessible outside your browser.

Permissions Explained

  • alarms – Schedules hourly breathing reminders.
  • notifications – Displays a brief reminder notification once per hour.
  • storage – Saves a local timestamp to avoid duplicate popups.
  • tabs – Opens the meditation page when you click a notification.
  • system.display – Reads screen size to position the popup in the corner.

Third Parties

Mindful Moments does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. There are no analytics, tracking scripts, or external services.
